BingX Launches AI Master, the World-First AI Crypto Trading Strategist

BingX has unveiled AI Master, the first AI-powered crypto trading strategist, marking a significant advancement in intelligent trading solutions.

On September 11, 2025, BingX, a leading cryptocurrency exchange and Web3 AI company, announced the launch of BingX AI Master, the world’s first AI-powered crypto trading strategist and the latest addition to the BingX AI suite of tools. This innovative product signifies a major milestone in BingX’s vision to enhance trading by making it more intelligent, accessible, and user-centric, thereby setting a new standard for innovation in digital asset management.

The introduction of AI Master comes at a crucial time, as the global demand for AI-driven financial solutions continues to rise. The AI trading platform market is projected to grow from $13.52 billion (AED 49 billion) in 2025 to approximately $69.95 billion (AED 256 billion) by 2034, highlighting the increasing significance of intelligent automation in the financial sector.

Within the MENA region, the UAE has emerged as a key driver of AI innovation, recently ranking fifth globally in Stanford University’s 2024 AI Index, surpassing several major economies. This recognition emphasizes the country’s ambitious vision to lead in transformative technologies. Beyond global accolades, the UAE’s strategic focus on AI is already reshaping critical sectors, including crypto trading, positioning the nation at the forefront of digital finance.

BingX AI Master aims to redefine the trading journey by guiding users from idea generation to simplified execution and transparent result reviews. By integrating the strategies of five top digital investors with advanced AI optimization, BingX empowers both novice and experienced traders to navigate the market with enhanced confidence and efficiency.

Key Features of BingX AI Master include:

  • Intelligent Strategy: 24/7 access to inspiration through real-time conversations with over 1,000 strategies inspired by five of the most influential masters.
  • Timely Investment Alerts: Instant notifications to keep users informed about the status, movements, and progress of their AI-powered strategies.
  • AI-Driven Backtesting: Continuous updates from AI-driven backtested data and strategy sources.
  • Simplified Execution: Streamlined buy and sell orders that adapt dynamically to market fluctuations, offering full user flexibility at every stage.
  • Transparent AI Review: Clear performance reviews, results, and records that enable users to refine their strategies and enhance their skills through AI-optimized insights.

“BingX AI Master is not just a trading tool—it’s a trading strategist designed to bring clarity, discipline, and confidence to every user’s journey,” stated Vivien Lin, Chief Product Officer at BingX. “With AI Master, we are demonstrating that intelligent trading can be accessible to all, not just seasoned professionals. This launch embodies BingX’s mission to redefine accessibility and create unprecedented user experiences in digital asset trading—building the first truly All-in-AI exchange.”

To celebrate the launch of BingX AI Master, BingX is hosting a head-to-head trading competition with a prize pool of 3,000,000 USDT. For the first time, users can compete directly against BingX AI Master, adding a competitive edge and interactive gameplay to the platform. Users can easily sign up to start earning rewards, with additional prizes available through task-based lucky draws, trading volume contests, and the AI 1v1 Arena.

With the introduction of BingX AI Master, BingX continues to expand its suite of AI intelligence products, reinforcing its position as the first exchange to fully integrate AI across the trading experience. Looking ahead, BingX plans to further enhance its AI product portfolio following the milestone release of BingX AI Master and BingX AI Bingo, merging cutting-edge intelligence with a user-first philosophy to shape the future of global trading.
