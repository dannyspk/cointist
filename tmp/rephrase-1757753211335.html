August Funding Decline

August saw a 30% drop in funding for crypto protocols, bringing the total to nearly $2 billion. This decrease highlights the challenges faced by the sector amid changing market dynamics.

Third Quarter Funding Overview

Despite the dip in August, total funding for the third quarter reached $4.57 billion within just two months, surpassing the previous quarter's total of $4.54 billion. This indicates a robust interest in the crypto space overall, even as certain areas face challenges.

Shift Towards Established Projects

According to market analyst Daan Crypto Trades, there has been a noticeable shift in investment focus towards established projects instead of new-chain launches. This trend demonstrates a preference for reliability amid market uncertainties.

Investment in DeFi and Other Sectors

While decentralized finance (DeFi) continues to be a major player, other sectors are also attracting considerable investment. Key highlights include:

  • Infrastructure solutions
  • Payment technologies
  • Rain's successful raise of $58 million

Regulatory Changes in South Korea

In a significant move, South Korea's Ministry of SMEs and Startups lifted a long-standing venture capital funding ban on September 16, 2025. This change aims to foster a transparent ecosystem while encouraging capital influx to blockchain-focused projects.

Current Market Landscape

The total cryptocurrency market capitalization currently stands at $3.95 trillion. This figure underscores the ongoing interest in the crypto sector, despite recent funding fluctuations.
