Record Active Addresses

On September 12, OKX's X Layer registered a remarkable 71,400 active addresses, marking the highest number of active users in the network's history.

Surging Address Growth

The total address count on the X Layer has now exceeded 4 million, highlighting the platform's expanding user base and increased adoption.

Impressive DEX Fees

In terms of financial performance, the network has generated nearly $1.0 million in DEX fees. This performance underscores the platform's growing significance in the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem.

Market Positioning

OKX currently holds the second position among Ethereum Virtual Machine-compatible networks in terms of DEX fees, following the BNB Chain. This positioning reflects the network's competitive edge and popularity among users.

Significance of Growth

The increases in both addresses and DEX fees signal substantial advancement for OKX's X Layer platform. These achievements are indicative of rising user engagement and heightened activity within the network, showcasing its potential in the evolving DeFi landscape.
