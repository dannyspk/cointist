Insights from Jordi Visser

Jordi Visser, a prominent figure on Wall Street, projects an increase in Bitcoin investments from traditional financial institutions by the close of 2023. He believes that these adjustments will better position portfolios for the forthcoming year.

Institutional Intentions to Expand Crypto Holdings

A recent survey conducted by Coinbase in partnership with EY-Parthenon revealed that an impressive 83% of institutional investors intend to increase their crypto allocations by 2025. This indicates a growing confidence in cryptocurrencies as part of investment strategies.

Predicted Bitcoin Inflows

According to Bitwise, significant inflows into Bitcoin are on the horizon. The firm estimates that Bitcoin could see:

  • $120 billion in inflows by 2025
  • $300 billion by 2026

Spot Bitcoin ETF Performance

In the United States, spot Bitcoin ETFs have experienced remarkable activity, showcasing $2.33 billion in net inflows recently. Cumulatively, these investments have reached a substantial total of $56.79 billion since January 2024.

Corporate Bitcoin Holdings

Publicly traded companies now possess approximately $117.03 billion in Bitcoin across their balance sheets. This accumulation underscores the growing institutional interest in the cryptocurrency space.

Optimism Surrounding Bitcoin's Price Trends

Visser has expressed optimism regarding Bitcoin's price trends, noting positive chart patterns and several mini breakouts in the crypto market. His insights reflect a broader sense of confidence among traditional investors about Bitcoin's potential trajectory.
