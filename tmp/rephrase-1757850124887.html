Invitation to Global Crypto Firms

Pakistan is encouraging international virtual asset service providers to apply for licenses under the regulatory framework of the Pakistan Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (PVARA). This move marks a significant step towards integrating global crypto firms into the Pakistani market.

Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for licensing, firms must be recognized by established regulators such as the SEC and FCA. This requirement ensures that only reputable organizations can operate within Pakistan's evolving crypto landscape.

Submission Requirements

Expressions of Interest (EoIs) must include comprehensive company profiles along with compliance records. These submissions will be crucial in assessing the firms' legitimacy and capacity to operate responsibly in the local context.

Focus on Transparency and Compliance

The initiative is designed not only to foster growth in the crypto sector but also to promote transparency and tackle concerns related to illicit finance. By establishing a clear regulatory framework, Pakistan aims to provide a secure environment for digital asset transactions.

Crypto Market Growth

According to Chainalysis' 2025 index, Pakistan's crypto market ranks third globally in adoption. This impressive standing highlights the increasing interest and participation of the local population in digital assets.

Government Initiatives and Concerns

In addition to welcoming international firms, plans have been announced for a government-led Bitcoin Strategic Reserve, indicating a proactive approach towards cryptocurrency. However, the International Monetary Fund (IMF) has raised concerns about the potential use of surplus electricity for crypto mining operations, suggesting a need for careful consideration in this area.
