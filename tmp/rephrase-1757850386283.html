Invitation for Licensing Applications

The Pakistan Virtual Asset Regulatory Authority (PVARA) is now open for applications from global crypto firms seeking to establish operations in the country. This move aims to create a structured regulatory environment for cryptocurrencies.

Targeted Participation

The call for Expressions of Interest (EoIs) specifically targets:

  • Leading cryptocurrency exchanges
  • Virtual asset service providers (VASPs)

Eligible firms must hold licenses from reputable regulatory bodies, such as the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the UK Financial Conduct Authority.

Application Requirements

Companies interested in applying need to submit comprehensive details, including:

  • Company profiles
  • Current licenses
  • Proposed services
  • Compliance records

Goals of PVARA

PVARA is designed not only to regulate the crypto market but also to enhance financial transparency across the sector. Additionally, it aims to combat illicit financial activities while promoting growth within the fintech ecosystem.
