Invitation for Licensing

The government of Pakistan has officially opened its doors for global crypto firms to seek licenses via the PVARA. This initiative represents a significant step towards integrating international cryptocurrency operations within the country.

Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for licensing, companies must be recognized by reputable regulators such as:

  • The United States Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
  • The United Kingdom's Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

These stringent requirements ensure that only credible firms enter the Pakistani market.

Application Requirements

Firms interested in applying must submit comprehensive company profiles alongside their proposed services. This information will be crucial in assessing the suitability of the applicants for the Pakistani fintech landscape.

Regulatory Framework

The licensing initiative is governed by the Virtual Assets Ordinance 2025. By adhering to international regulatory standards, the framework aims to foster a secure environment for innovation in the fintech sector while simultaneously aiming to curb illicit financial activities.
