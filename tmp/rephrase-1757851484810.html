Encouraging International Participation

In a significant step towards integrating into the global cryptocurrency market, Pakistan is encouraging international crypto companies to apply for licenses under the framework established by the Pakistan Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (PVARA).

Compliance with Global Standards

Bilal bin Saqib, a prominent figure in this initiative, has highlighted the necessity of compliance with international standards to ensure the legitimacy and security of crypto operations within the country.

Eligibility Criteria for Applicants

To qualify for licensing, applicants must meet specific eligibility criteria:

  • Firms must be licensed by recognized global regulatory bodies.
  • Submission of detailed company profiles is required.
  • A comprehensive business model must be provided.

The Goals of PVARA

PVARA is designed not only to thwart illicit financial activities but also to foster innovation in fintech and promote opportunities in tokenization.

Global Crypto Adoption in Pakistan

According to the 2025 index by Chainalysis, Pakistan ranks third in the world for cryptocurrency adoption, reflecting a strong public interest in digital assets.

Future Infrastructure Plans

Looking ahead, the country plans to leverage its surplus electricity for Bitcoin mining and explore initiatives related to artificial intelligence, showcasing a commitment to advancing its digital economy.
