Invitation for Expressions of Interest

As part of its strategy to enhance the digital financial landscape, Pakistan's PVARA has issued a call for Expressions of Interest (EoIs) from global crypto firms. This initiative is expected to cultivate partnerships that contribute to a robust and transparent financial future.

Eligibility Criteria

Firms interested in participating must meet specific eligibility criteria, including:

  • Licensing by reputable global regulators such as the US SEC and the UK Financial Conduct Authority.

Submission Requirements

To apply, companies must submit comprehensive documentation that includes:

  • Detailed company profiles.
  • A description of proposed services.
  • Records demonstrating compliance.
  • A business model tailored for the Pakistani market.

Emerging Crypto Landscape

Pakistan's commitment to embracing cryptocurrency is reflected in its ranking as the third position in the 2025 Global Crypto Adoption Index. This ranking underscores the country's growing significance in the global crypto market.

Future Developments

In addition to regulatory initiatives, Pakistan plans to harness its surplus electricity for Bitcoin mining and the establishment of AI centers. However, this ambition comes with challenges, particularly concerning the International Monetary Fund's (IMF) apprehensions about energy subsidies for the cryptocurrency sector.
