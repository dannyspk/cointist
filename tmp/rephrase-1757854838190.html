Opening Applications for Crypto Firms

The Pakistan Virtual Asset Regulatory Authority (PVARA) has officially commenced the application process for international virtual asset service providers (VASPs). This move is part of the country's broader strategy to foster a regulated environment for cryptocurrency operations.

Submission Requirements

To gain entry into the Pakistani market, VASPs must submit Expressions of Interest that meet specific criteria:

  • Firms must hold licenses from recognized global regulators such as the US SEC and the UK Financial Conduct Authority.
  • Submissions should include comprehensive company profiles and robust compliance records.
  • Details of specific business models tailored for the Pakistani market are essential.

Pakistan's Crypto Adoption Landscape

According to Chainalysis' 2025 Global Crypto Adoption Index, Pakistan ranks third globally in cryptocurrency adoption. This notable position underscores the increasing interest and investment in digital assets among the Pakistani populace.

Future Initiatives

In addition to the new licensing initiative, Pakistan is planning further advancements in its cryptocurrency strategy. This includes the establishment of a government-led Bitcoin Strategic Reserve and the allocation of resources specifically dedicated to crypto mining operations.
