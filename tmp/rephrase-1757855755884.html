Current Market Position

Despite several attempts to surpass the significant resistance level of $115,892, Bitcoin's price has remained below this threshold. This consolidation phase has raised questions about the cryptocurrency's near-term trajectory.

On-Chain Indicators and Market Sentiment

Recent on-chain metrics indicate a developing bullish momentum in the market. Notably, there are now a record 54.37 million wallet addresses holding non-zero balances, a clear sign of increasing interest among investors.

Profitability of Circulating Supply

An impressive 93.6% of Bitcoin's circulating supply is currently showing profits, a figure that notably exceeds the long-term average of 75%. This high profitability level suggests a prevailing optimism among holders.

  • Analyst Crypto Avails highlights that such profitability could further bolster market confidence.

Potential Price Movements

Should Bitcoin successfully breach the $115,892 resistance level, analysts forecast a potential rally that could see prices rise to $119,367 or even reach as high as $122,190. However, in the face of increasing bearish pressure, there is a possibility that Bitcoin's price might decline to approximately $111,961.
