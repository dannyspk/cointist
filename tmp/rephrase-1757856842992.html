Joao Wedson, the CEO of Alphractal, has indicated that Bitcoin could experience a significant price increase should it break through the $118,000 threshold. This price point is considered critical for a bullish trend to emerge.

Resistance Levels

The $117,000 level has been identified as an important resistance, shaped by various on-chain metrics. These metrics include:

  • CVDD Channel: This tool calculates historical price support and identifies risk areas based on data related to coin destruction.
  • Fibonacci-Adjusted Market Mean Price: This indicator further establishes market trends and levels of support.

Predictive Accuracy of On-Chain Metrics

Historically, both the CVDD Channel and Fibonacci-Adjusted Market Mean Price have been reliable in forecasting Bitcoin's support and resistance levels, making them critical for traders and investors alike.

Investor Recommendations

Analysts recommend that investors remain vigilant and await a definitive move above $118,000. Such a breakout would likely signal the resurgence of bullish sentiment in the market.
