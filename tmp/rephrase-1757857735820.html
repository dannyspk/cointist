Invitation for Licensing Applications

Pakistan has announced its decision to welcome international crypto firms by allowing them to submit applications for operating licenses under the new PVARA framework. This initiative seeks to enhance the regulatory environment for digital currencies within the country.

Submission Criteria

The government is focusing on licensed entities from established regulatory bodies, emphasizing the need for compliance in this emerging sector.

  • Firms must demonstrate adherence to regulatory standards
  • Security measures are a primary consideration
  • Eligibility criteria are outlined in detail within the PVARA documentation

Goals of the Initiative

This initiative is part of Pakistan's broader strategy to cultivate a transparent digital financial future while simultaneously tackling concerns related to illicit finance.

Crypto Market Recognition

In a significant achievement, Pakistan's crypto market has been acknowledged, ranking 3rd in the 2025 Global Crypto Adoption Index. This recognition highlights the country's growing engagement with digital currencies.

Governmental Plans for Bitcoin

With an eye on the future, the Pakistani government is also developing plans for a Bitcoin Strategic Reserve. Additionally, there are provisions for allocating surplus electricity specifically for cryptocurrency mining operations.

IMF Concerns

However, these developments are not without controversy. The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has voiced concerns regarding the environmental implications of using electricity for crypto mining, urging caution in resource allocation.
