Invitation to Global Crypto Businesses

The government of Pakistan has launched a new initiative that encourages global crypto exchanges and virtual asset service providers (VASPs) to apply for operational licenses. This move is facilitated through the Pakistan Virtual Asset Regulatory Authority (PVARA).

Eligibility Criteria for Applicants

To qualify for licensing, firms must meet specific criteria, which includes being duly licensed by well-regarded global regulatory bodies. This requirement is essential to ensure that only reputable companies can participate in Pakistan's growing crypto market.

Application Submission Requirements

Interested companies are required to submit detailed applications that include:

  • Company information
  • Proposed services
  • Records of compliance with international regulations

Framework Objectives

This new licensing framework is designed not only to boost fintech opportunities within the country but also to ensure that all operations adhere to global compliance standards. By doing so, Pakistan aims to cultivate a secure and reliable environment for crypto activities.

Pakistan's Crypto Adoption Ranking

As evidence of its growing crypto landscape, Pakistan currently ranks third in the Chainalysis 2025 Global Crypto Adoption Index. This ranking highlights the increasing interest and participation in cryptocurrency among the nation's population.

Recent Government Initiatives

In addition to the licensing initiative, the Pakistani government has introduced several strategic measures to foster the crypto economy, including:

  • The establishment of a Bitcoin Strategic Reserve
  • Plans for Bitcoin mining operations
  • Development of AI centers focused on cryptocurrency and blockchain technology

These initiatives reflect Pakistan's commitment to integrating cryptocurrency as a vital component of its technological and economic growth.
