Inflation Trends

The most recent data on the Consumer Price Index (CPI) revealed a month-over-month increase of 0.4%. This outcome exceeded the anticipated 0.3% forecast, signaling escalating inflationary pressures.

Job Market Challenges

In a significant revision, it was revealed that nearly 1 million fewer jobs were created over the past year, marking the largest drop in U.S. history. The job creation figures for August reported merely 22,000 new jobs, while the unemployment rate rose to 4.3%.
