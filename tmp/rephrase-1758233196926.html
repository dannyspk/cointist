Deal terms and strategic backers

Brera Holdings confirmed the $300 million private investment in public equity (PIPE) was oversubscribed, attracting a mix of institutional and regional strategic investors. Backers included the Solana Foundation, Cathie Wood's Ark Invest, UAE-based Pulsar Group and RockawayX, a lineup that underlines strong support from both crypto-native organizations and global asset managers.

The company said the placement will finance Solmate's initial infrastructure buildout and token accumulation plan, while leaving room to retain and operate Brera's existing sports and media businesses. Management framed the capital raise as a foundation for a broader pivot toward crypto infrastructure and market participation rather than a full divestiture of legacy assets.

Leadership, governance and SOL accumulation

Marco Santori was named CEO of the newly rebranded Solmate, and the company announced that Dr. Arthur Laffer and Viktor Fischer will sit on Solmate's board. The appointments signal a mix of crypto-focused legal and industry experience alongside policy and commercial perspectives intended to guide the company through regulatory and market shifts.
