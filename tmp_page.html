Stablecoins & Payments: Move Money Fast and Cheap

Xinr
Luca De Santis
Track 3 - Applications
Last updated: Sep 3, 2025

DEFINITION

Stablecoins target price stability (usually $1) and power low-friction payments. Designs include fiat-backed, crypto-collateralized, and algorithmic/hybrid models.

Designs

  • Fiat-backed: off-chain reserves, attestation cadence, redeemability.
  • Crypto-collateralized: on-chain reserves, overcollateralization, liquidation engines.
  • Algorithmic/hybrid: supply rules + collateral; higher design risk.

Payments rails

On/off-ramps, merchant acceptance, L2 fees/latency, and compliance endpoints (KYC/AML, travel rule).

Metrics & KPIs

Peg deviation
Median price vs $1 over time
Redeemability
Mint/burn windows and frictions
Reserve quality
Asset mix, duration, transparency
On-chain velocity
Tx volume / circulating supply
Acceptance
Merchants & wallets that support it
Settlement cost
Avg fee & time-to-finality

Operational notes

  • Plan for chain outages or L2 sequencer downtime.
  • Monitor bridge/liquidity fragmentation across chains.
